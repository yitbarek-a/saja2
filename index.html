About Saja General Hospital

Saja General Hospital was established in 2017 with a vision to provide exceptional healthcare services to our community. In October 2025, we were upgraded to a General Hospital, expanding our capacity and services to better serve our patients.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality medical care with excellence and compassion, ensuring every patient receives personalized attention and the best possible treatment outcomes. We combine state-of-the-art technology with a patient-centered approach to healthcare.

Major Hospital Wards

Our specialized inpatient care units

Medical Ward

የአዋቂ ተኝቶ ህክምና ክፍል

Comprehensive care for adults with medical conditions including diabetes, hypertension, respiratory diseases, and infectious diseases.

Pediatrics Ward

የህፃናት ተኝቶ ህክምና ክፍል

Surgical Ward

የቀዶ ጥገና ህክምና ክፍል

Post-operative care for patients recovering from various surgical procedures with advanced monitoring systems.

OBGYN Ward
