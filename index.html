About Saja General Hospital

Saja General Hospital was established in 2017 with a vision to provide exceptional healthcare services to our community. In October 2025, we were upgraded to a General Hospital, expanding our capacity and services to better serve our patients.

Our mission is to deliver high-quality medical care with excellence and compassion, ensuring every patient receives personalized attention and the best possible treatment outcomes. We combine state-of-the-art technology with a patient-centered approach to healthcare.

Major Hospital Wards

Our specialized inpatient care units

Medical Ward

የአዋቂ ተኝቶ ህክምና ክፍል

Comprehensive care for adults with medical conditions including diabetes, hypertension, respiratory diseases, and infectious diseases. Our medical ward features modern monitoring equipment and specialized nursing care.

Pediatrics Ward

የህፃናት ተኝቶ ህክምና ክፍል

Child-friendly environment for infants, children, and adolescents. Our pediatric specialists provide care for common childhood illnesses, infections, and chronic conditions with a focus on family-centered care.

Surgical Ward

የቀዶ ጥገና ህክምና ክፍል

Post-operative care for patients recovering from various surgical procedures. Our surgical ward is equipped with advanced monitoring systems and staffed by experienced surgical nurses.

OBGYN Ward

የማህፀንና ፅንስ ህክምና ክፍል

Specialized care for women during pregnancy, childbirth, and postpartum period. Our OBGYN ward also provides gynecological services in a comfortable and private environment.

Hospital-based Longevity & Lifestyle Medicine Program (LLMP)

Also known as "Center for Healthy Aging & Lifestyle Medicine"

Program Components:

  • Personalized Preventive Care: Tailored health plans based on individual risk factors and genetics.
  • Community & Patient Education: Workshops and resources for healthy living and disease prevention.
  • Lifestyle-Based Disease Reversal: Evidence-based approaches to reverse chronic conditions through lifestyle changes.

Our LLMP program focuses on holistic health approaches that address the root causes of chronic diseases and promote healthy aging through personalized lifestyle interventions.

Dr. Yohannes Tadesse
Program Founder & Director
